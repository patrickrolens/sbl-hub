Springfield Battle League · Season 1

Rules & Resources

Everything you need to draft, battle, and compete this season.

The Springfield Battle League is a VGC Draft League (4v4 doubles) played in Pokémon Showdown. Each player drafts a team from a shared pool and plays one match per week across a regular season, followed by a postseason bracket.

Format: [Gen 8] VGC 2020 — select this when building your team and challenging your opponent. Matches are reported by Friday each week.

Signups: The league begins with signups! Interested players should post in the signups channel in the Discord or DM jdawgin13 to be added to the roster.

01Match Format▾

All matches are double battles played on Pokémon Showdown. Each week you play your assigned opponent in a Best of 3.

Record your differential after each game (how many Pokémon you have remaining when you win). You may also record KOs for individual leaderboard tracking.

02The Draft▾

All players draft from the same pool. The draft runs snake-style: picks go down the order, then reverse at the end and come back up, repeating until every player has a full team of ten Pokémon.

Roster requirements
  • Every team must include exactly one Gigantamax Pokémon. Only this Pokémon may Gigantamax during matches.
  • Required minimums for the remaining picks: 1 Tier 1, 2 Tier 2, 2 Tier 3, 1 Tier 4. The remaining 3 picks are flexible and may be from any tier.
  • Flex picks may not push any single tier above twice its required minimum (e.g. Tier 2 requires 2, so you may not draft more than 4 Tier 2 total).
  • Only Pokémon currently in Sword & Shield may be drafted.

04In-Match Rules▾
Team selection
  • 4v4 double battle format. Each player brings 6 Pokémon per match and selects 4 to use each game.
  • The team of 6 you bring must be used for all games in that match — no changes between games.
  • You may pick a different set of 4 from your 6 each game, but moves, items, abilities, stats, and natures cannot change.
Dynamax & Gigantamax
  • Any Pokémon on your team may Dynamax during a match.
  • Only your one drafted Gigantamax Pokémon may Gigantamax — no other Pokémon may use G-Max moves, even if they are ordinarily capable of it.
Banned moves & items
  • Moves that exclusively raise evasion or lower accuracy — Sand Attack, Double Team, Minimize, and similar — are banned.
  • Duplicate items are not allowed.
Forfeits
  • A forfeited match results in a loss, a −2 game differential, and a −4 K/D for the forfeiting player.
05Trades & Free Agency▾

Players can adjust their drafted teams through Free Agency swaps (dropping a Pokémon for an undrafted one) or trades with other players.

  • Each player may make one swap per week. The replacement must be from the same tier as the Pokémon being dropped.
  • Trades between players must also be within the same tier.
  • All transactions take effect the following week — e.g. a pickup announced in Week 3 is usable starting Week 4.
  • Announce all swaps and trades in the #sbl-transactions channel on Discord.

07Competitive Integrity▾
  • Sabotage of matches — match fixing, intentionally losing, or otherwise undermining the league's competitive integrity — is not allowed.
  • No coaching during a set.

Violations are punished based on their nature and the commissioner's judgment, ranging from a warning to removal from the league.

Questions or edge cases not covered here are resolved by the commissioner.
