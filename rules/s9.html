Springfield Battle League · Season 9

Rules & Resources

Everything you need to draft, battle, and compete this season.

The Springfield Battle League is a VGC Draft League (4v4 doubles) played in Pokémon Showdown. Each player drafts a team using a point budget and plays one match per week, facing ten different opponents across the season.

Format: [Gen 9] 4v4 Doubles Draft — select this when building your team and challenging your opponent. Matches are reported by 11:59 pm Sunday each week.

Signups: Interested players should DM jdawgin13 to be added to the roster.

02Seeding & Schedule▾

Once the roster is finalized, players are seeded based on performance in past SBL seasons. Where data on a player is limited, the commissioner determines seeding from the available information.

Seeding is used to build the schedule. Because of the number of entrants, not everyone plays everyone — instead, seeding gives every player a roughly equally challenging season. Each player plays one match per week over ten weeks, facing ten different opponents.

03The Draft▾

After seeding, the draft order is randomly decided and announced. All players draft from the same pool in snake-style until every player has a full team of 10 Pokémon.

Draft order swaps
  • Two players may swap positions in the draft order by mutual agreement. Each player may swap only once.
  • A player who swaps with someone who later drops out is returned to their original position.
Live draft & absences
  • The commissioner will attempt to find a time when all players can draft live. Otherwise, drafting proceeds gradually through Discord.
  • If a player who agreed to attend is absent, they automatically draft the highest-usage Pokémon (per Pikalytics) from the highest tier they still need.
Opening swap window: For the first 24–48 hours after the draft (the exact length is announced), players may make unlimited trades and Free Agency swaps. These are effective Week 1.
04Budget & Tiers▾

Each team has a 310-point budget to spend on 10 Pokémon. Every Pokémon belongs to a tier with a fixed point cost (see the Tiers tab).

Tera Captains

Players may designate up to 2 Pokémon as Tera Captains. A Tera Captain can Terastallize during battle. Designating a Pokémon as a Tera Captain costs additional points — the exact cost per Pokémon is shown in the Tiers tab. Each Tera Captain designation counts as a transaction against the season limit.

Total drafted points (including any Tera Captain costs) may not exceed 310. Any transaction that would push a player over budget is invalid.
05In-Match Rules▾
Team selection
  • 4v4 double battle format. Each player brings 6 Pokémon per match and selects 4 to use each game.
  • Once your first game begins, you must use the same team of 6 for every game in that match.
  • You may pick a different set of 4 each game, but moves, items, abilities, stats, and natures cannot change between games.
Banned Pokémon, abilities & moves
Banned: Commander (ability), OHKO moves (Fissure, Guillotine, Horn Drill, Sheer Cold), Moody (ability), and Dark Void (move) are not permitted.
Banned moves & items
  • Moves that exclusively raise evasion or lower accuracy — Sand Attack, Double Team, Minimize, and similar — are banned (including Brightpowder and Mud-Slap).
  • Duplicate items are not allowed.
Timer
  • Any player may request a turn timer at any point during a match. By default, the timer is 2 minutes per turn. Players may agree to a different time; if they can't agree, 2 minutes applies.
  • The timer should be kept by a third party (preferably jdawg or Shrug).
Forfeits
  • A forfeited match results in a loss, a −2 game differential, and a −4 K/D for the forfeiting player.
06Trades & Free Agency▾

Players may make Free Agency swaps (dropping a Pokémon for an undrafted one), trades with other players, or Tera Captain changes. All three count against the same limit.

  • Players are allowed up to 10 FA swaps and trades total for the season. Tera Captain changes each count as one transaction against this limit.
  • Players may not trade past Week 8.
  • All transactions take effect the following week.
  • Announce all swaps and trades in the #sbl-transactions channel on Discord.

08All-Star Match & Awards▾
All-Star Match

Players are invited to vote for which Pokémon were the true All Stars of the season. These are split into two groups, team captains are chosen, and players pick sides schoolyard-style. Both teams build and face off in a best-of-five set. Pokémon can be swapped each game, but their movesets, stats, etc. cannot change. Entirely optional but worth joining.

09Competitive Integrity▾
  • Sabotage of matches — match fixing, intentionally losing, or otherwise violating the competitive integrity of the league — is not allowed.
  • Spectators are welcome but should avoid giving advice or information, intentionally or accidentally, to players in ongoing matches.
  • Helping players prepare for upcoming matches is fine — but don't build a complete Poképaste for another player. The sharing restriction applies specifically to ongoing matches.

Violations are punished based on their nature and the commissioner's judgment. Reach out to the commissioner for any clarification.
