Springfield Battle League · Season 5

Rules & Resources

Everything you need to draft, battle, and compete this season.

The Springfield Battle League is a VGC Draft League (4v4 doubles) played in Pokémon Showdown. Each player drafts a team using a point budget and plays one match per week across a regular season split into two leagues, followed by a postseason bracket.

Format: [Gen 9] VGC 2023 Series 1 — select this when building your team and challenging your opponent. Matches are reported by 11:59 pm Sunday each week.

Record your differential after each game (how many Pokémon you have remaining when you win) and your K/D for the individual leaderboards.

02Seeding & Leagues▾

Season 5 runs as two separate leagues. Players are divided between the leagues based on seeding from prior SBL seasons. Each league plays a full round-robin regular season within itself.

The top 5 records from each league advance to the combined postseason playoff bracket. The bottom 6 from each league are eliminated after the regular season.

03The Draft▾

Each league drafts from its own separate pool using a snake draft. A Pokémon drafted in one league is still available in the other league. Each team drafts 10 Pokémon within a 115-point budget.

Draft order swaps
  • Two players may swap positions in the draft order by mutual agreement. Each player may swap only once.
Absences
  • If a player who agreed to attend is absent, they automatically draft the highest-usage Pokémon (per Pikalytics) for their pick.

05In-Match Rules▾
Team selection
  • 4v4 double battle format. Each player brings 6 Pokémon per match and selects 4 to use each game.
  • The team of 6 you bring must remain the same for all games in a match.
Banned moves & items
  • Moves that raise evasion or lower accuracy — Sand Attack, Double Team, Minimize, and similar — are banned.
  • Duplicate items are not allowed.
Timer
  • Either player may request a turn timer at any point during a match. The default is 2 minutes per turn unless both players agree to a different time.
Forfeits
  • A forfeited match results in a loss, a −2 game differential, and a −4 K/D for the forfeiting player.
06Trades & Free Agency▾
Post-draft window
  • For the first 24 hours after the draft, all swaps and trades are unlimited and take effect for Week 1.
Regular season (Weeks 1–9)
  • Each player may make up to 2 Free Agency swaps per week, with a maximum of 10 total FA swaps for the season.
  • Player-to-player trades are unlimited and do not count against the FA swap limit.
  • All transactions take effect the following week.
Pre-playoffs
  • Players may make 4 additional swaps before the postseason begins.

Announce all swaps and trades in the #sbl-transactions channel on Discord.

09Competitive Integrity▾
  • Sabotage of matches — match fixing, intentionally losing, or otherwise undermining the league's competitive integrity — is not allowed.
  • No coaching during a set.

Violations are punished based on their nature and the commissioner's judgment, ranging from a warning to removal from the league.
